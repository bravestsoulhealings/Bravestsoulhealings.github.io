About Me

My name is Natalie. I am an intuitive faith healer. Growing up, I was very shy and nervous. I often isolated myself and struggled with severe sadness, panic attacks, and anxiety. Despite this, I always remained hopeful that things would get better.

I felt a deep connection to my ancestors, my Guardian Angel, the Archangels, angels, and God throughout my life. There were nights I could not sleep, and I would pray and speak to God until I fell asleep. I grew up in a Catholic household, attending Mass as a child, but as I got older, I distanced myself from my faith.

Between 2020 and 2023, everything felt very heavy. I searched for natural ways to heal my sadness, afraid to take medication, hoping there was another path. I explored tarot, reiki, and mediumship, even taking classes—but I always felt empty afterward.

In May of 2023, feeling hopeless and defeated, I cried out to God, asking for guidance and peace. That is when I searched for spiritual healing and found a place that felt different from anything I had seen before. I booked an appointment without hesitation.

That experience changed my life. For the first time, the sadness I had always carried was gone. I felt free, relieved, and lighter. My faith was restored, and I knew this was my calling—to be an instrument of God and help others in need.
