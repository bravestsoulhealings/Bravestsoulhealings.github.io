About Me

My name is Natalie. I am an intuitive faith healer. Growing up, I was very shy and often struggled with deep sadness, anxiety, and panic attacks. Even in my most difficult moments, I held onto hope that healing was possible.

I have always felt a strong spiritual connection to God, angels, and divine protection. Prayer was my refuge. Though I drifted from my faith for a time, my heart never fully left it.

During my darkest season, I searched for healing and peace. I explored many paths, but nothing filled the emptiness I felt — until I cried out to God completely and sincerely for guidance.

That prayer led me to spiritual healing. My first healing experience lifted a weight I had carried for years. I felt free, lighter, restored. My faith was renewed, and my calling became clear.

Today, I serve as an instrument of God’s healing and light for those who are suffering, overwhelmed, or spiritually burdened.

This is not just work — it is a calling.
